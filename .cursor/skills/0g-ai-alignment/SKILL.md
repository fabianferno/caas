# 0G AI Alignment Nodes

Specialized network participants that monitor and ensure safe, aligned AI behavior within the 0G ecosystem.

## When to Use

- Understanding AI safety monitoring in 0G
- Setting up or operating AI Alignment nodes
- Building applications that integrate with alignment verification
- Working with node sale / alignment node operations

## What AI Alignment Nodes Do

### AI Model Monitoring
- **Track Model Drift**: Detect when AI models deviate from expected behavior
- **Verify Outputs**: Ensure AI-generated results meet quality and safety standards
- **Monitor Performance**: Track AI system efficiency and accuracy over time
- **Flag Anomalies**: Alert the network to unusual or potentially harmful AI behavior

### Network Security
- **Identify Protocol Violations**: Detect nodes failing to follow rules
- **Report Malicious Behavior**: Flag potential attacks or bad actors
- **Maintain Ethical Standards**: Ensure AI operations align with community values
- **Support Governance**: Provide data for network governance decisions

## Future Capabilities

- Automated AI-powered oversight scaling with network growth
- Distributed community-driven governance
- Continuous learning alignment systems
- Global participation in AI safety monitoring
